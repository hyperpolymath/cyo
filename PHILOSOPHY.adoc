== The Problem: The Great Language Fatigue
The modern developer lives in a state of perpetual "Annoyance." We are forced into a cycle of destruction: to gain better technology, we are told we must kill our identity. To get memory safety, we must leave Python. To get formal proof, we must leave JavaScript. This is the **Social Anchor**—the fear that choosing a new tool means betraying our community and our "mother tongue."

== The Solution: The Federated Architecture
AffineScript is not a language; it is a **Federation**. It is designed as a "High-Integrity Hybrid" that respects the three pillars of developer psychology:

=== 1. The Face (The Language of Creation)
Your **Face** is your identity. Whether it is *JaffaScript*, *RattleScript*, or *LucidScript*, it is designed to feel like your "mother tongue."
* **Autonomy:** Each community owns its Face. They evolve it, sugar it, and live in it.
* **Identity:** You are not a "user of a dialect"; you are a citizen of a sovereign syntactic Republic.

=== 2. The Exo (The Language of Interconnection)
The **Exolanguage** (`.affinex`) is the shared "Universal Common Tongue." It is the **Shared Origin** that unifies the federation.
* **Exchange:** It is the "diplomatic passport" of code.
* **Legibility:** Even if you don't speak the "Face" of your neighbor, you can read their "Exo" and understand their logic perfectly. It is the "Soviet Origin" that provides a readable, intuitive foundation for everyone.

=== 3. The Core (The Language of Proof)
The **Core** is the stable, industrial engine. It is the "Common Good" that remains largely untouched, governed by the federation, providing:
* **Mathematical Proof:** Powered by Idris2.
* **Resource Integrity:** Affine types (at most once) tracked with mathematical rigor.
* **Stability:** While the Faces innovate and the Exo bridges, the Core remains the bedrock.

== The "At Most Once" Irony
The name **AffineScript** is our ironic manifesto. In an industry that constantly "double-spends" developer time by forcing rewrites, we believe in a system where you invest in your logic **exactly once**, and it remains interoperable across every "Face" we build.

== Our Ethics: Emancipation over Assimilation
We do not seek to "convert" developers. We seek to **emancipate** them.
* We acknowledge that **typing matters** (The Information Gap).
* We acknowledge that **community matters** (The Social Anchor).
* We acknowledge that **stability matters** (The Gimmick Filter).

By separating Identity (Face) from Origin (Core), we create a "Lossless Federation" where you lose nothing but your annoyances.

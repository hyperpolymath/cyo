# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

`cyo` exists because people enjoy the craft and should not be made to feel small
about where they are in it. We extend the same posture to this project's own
spaces: we pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of background, identity, or level of experience.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Meeting people where they are; no condescension about anyone's stack or skill.
- Honest, specific, kind feedback.
- Accepting responsibility and learning from mistakes.

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, demeaning comments, personal or
political attacks, and publishing others' private information.

## Enforcement

Report concerns to **j.d.a.jewell@open.ac.uk**. Reports are reviewed promptly
and handled confidentially. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject
contributions and participation that violate this code.
